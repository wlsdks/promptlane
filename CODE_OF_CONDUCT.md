# Code Of Conduct

promptlane uses a simple contribution standard:

- Be respectful and direct.
- Keep discussion focused on the project and the technical tradeoffs.
- Do not post private prompts, credentials, private paths, or personal data.
- Do not harass, threaten, or attack contributors.
- Assume maintainers may remove comments or close issues that create privacy,
  safety, or moderation risk.

Report conduct concerns privately through the contact channel listed in
`SUPPORT.md`.
